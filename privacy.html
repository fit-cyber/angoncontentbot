Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 28, 2026

App Name: AngonContentBot

1. Information We Collect

When you authorize AngonContentBot via TikTok OAuth, we collect:

  • Your TikTok display name and profile information
  • Permission to post content on your behalf (video.publish scope)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:

  • Post AI-generated video content to your TikTok account (when you request it)
  • Display your account information within the App

3. Data Storage

We store your OAuth tokens securely. We do not store your TikTok videos or personal content on our servers.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties, except as required by law.

5. Data Deletion

You may revoke the App's access at any time through your TikTok settings. Upon revocation, we delete all stored tokens and associated data.

6. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy at any time. We will notify users of significant changes.
